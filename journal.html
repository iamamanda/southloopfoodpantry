Amanda's Journal

My first impression of South Loop Community Church: It's so pretty. I volunteered twice this whole project and I really enjoyed this experience. The environment was light and cheerful, and the people there are really friendly. The clients were also very respectful. First time I went there, I was tasked with packing meat and making paper bags. It was simple tasks I didn't find to be that terrible at all. My second time, I was in the distribution room and was offering the clients fruits and meats. I enjoyed seeing the happy faces that walked in and joyfully taking their bag of goodies and knowing they'll have somethingt to eat for a few weeks.

South Loop Community Church: Food Pantry

This website is dedicated to sharing information about how South Loop Community Church run their section of the Chicago Food Depository. Throughout this site is information about their clever ways they have of distributing, and storing food, as well as how they recycle no-longer needed items, where they get their supplies, and technologies they use.

So sit back, relax because all the hard work has been done for you. (:

Author's Statement

The information contained in this website is based on the following experience:
>Amanda volunteered on 12/22/12 and 01/19/13 doing various tasks (storage, distribution room, packing, bag-making).
>Daja volunteered on 01/05/13 and 01/19/13 doing various tasks.

Amanda and Daja went about four times to gather information for this website. We did many tasks a typical volunteer will do and gathered our information mostly by simple observation. Our complete narrative will be told through the "Journal" link.

Where do the Food Come From?

South Loop Community Church receives its food from the Greater Chicago Food Depository, local donors, restaurants and regular civilians. The Pantry orders food from the Chicago Greater Food Depository twice a month and receives about 3,300 pounds of food per a month.

How does the food get here?

To get the food to the pantry a member uses their own car to go get the food. Occasionally, they order a van to get the food to and from the church.
